# 🃏 Card Collector Game

A personal full-stack card collecting game built for me and my friends. Players earn coins by playing minigames, spend them on themed card packs, and build out their collections — complete with rarities, shiny variants, and player-to-player trading.

---

## 📖 Overview

This was a private passion project inspired by trading card games and gacha mechanics. Two themed pack sets were available at launch: **Doctor Who** and **Blue Lock**. The goal was to give a small group of friends a fun shared experience — opening packs, chasing shinies, and trading duplicates.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🃏 Card System

- Cards organised into **themed sets** (Doctor Who, Blue Lock)  
- Multiple **rarity tiers** — common through to ultra rare  
- **Shiny variants** with unique visual treatment for each card  
- All card and pack data stored in **JSON files** for easy extensibility

### 🛍️ Pack Shop

- Players visit the in-game shop to purchase packs using earned coins  
- Packs have defined rarity weightings that determine pull rates  
- Both Doctor Who and Blue Lock packs available at launch

### 🎮 Minigames

- **Football Shootout** — pick a direction to shoot; the goalkeeper randomly defends one direction. Score to earn coins, miss and walk away empty-handed.  
- Minigames serve as the primary coin-earning mechanic

### 🔄 Multiplayer Trading

- Players can **trade cards directly** with one another  
- Encourages cooperation and collection completion between friends

---

## 🗂️ Data Structure

All game data is stored in flat **JSON files**, including:

- Card definitions (name, rarity, shiny status, theme/set)  
- Pack contents and rarity weightings  
- Player inventories and coin balances  
- Trade history
